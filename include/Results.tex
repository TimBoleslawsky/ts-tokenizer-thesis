\chapter{Experimental Results}

Within this chapter the results for the experiments for phase one and two are presented. The main goal is to answer research questions one and two. 

\section{Phase 1 Experiments}

First, the efficacy of the two baseline models presented in Chapter~\ref{chap:methods} when applied to IoT and automotive data is determined. For this, both these baseline models were implemented according to the architecture outlined in Chapter~\ref{chap:methods}.

\subsection{The Problem with Continuous-latent Autoencoders}

The TCN-RNN continuous-latent autoencoder is first evaluated on the appliances energy dataset used by Zheng and Zhang~\cite{Zheng2023, Candanedo2017}. The use case the authors present in their paper uses two channels: ``Appliances'' and ``Windspeed''. The training goal is to compress and reconstruct these two signals. The training setup for this first experiment is purposefully kept as close as possible to the authors setup.
